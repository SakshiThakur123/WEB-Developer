MY Hobby

Hobby is Something that you do regularly for pleasure in your free time.

My hobby is dancing, as it gives me joy and happiness.Dancing is not only about performing moves, but it is a way to express your emotions.Since my childhood days, I grasped the art of dancing.That's because dancing, like many physical activities, releases hormones known as endorphins. These chemicals are directly responsible for making you feel happy and relaxed.

I dance because there's no greater feeling in the world than moving a piece of music and letting the rest of the world disappear. Some of my happiest moments have been dancing.

Why I love Dance:-

  1. It lets me feel free
  2. It lifts my mood
  3. It makes me feel good
  4. I have fun learning new moves
  5. Gives me energy

Benefits of Dancing:-

  • improved condition of your heart and lungs.
  • better coordination, agility and flexibility.
  • weight management.
  • improve your mood while you learn, move, and perform
  • Dance is an effective type of exercise that raises your heart rate and works your muscles.
  • Dancing is a great way to reduce stress.
  • create a feeling of comfort, relaxation, fun and power.
